CryGetter

CryGetter is a computational tool that aims to perform automatic retrieval of Cry protein data.

Welcome to CryGetter project page!

CryGetter is a computational tool used to automaticaly retrieve CryProtein data and to offer means to analyse then. It is being developed by David Buzatto, as a part of his Ph.D. project, with the advisement of Prof. Sônia Marli Zinagaretti.

We want to thank IFSP - Instituto Federal de Educação, Ciência e Tecnologia de São Paulo for the financial support.
